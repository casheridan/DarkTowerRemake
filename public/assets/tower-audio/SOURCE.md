# Original tower audio captures

These 19 WAV files were downloaded from [The Dark Tower Page](https://well-of-souls.com/tower/)
on 2026-07-18. The archive credits Death Lock with recording and contributing the sounds.
They are captures of the original appliance (22,050 Hz, mono, 8-bit WAV), not newly composed
music. No reuse license is stated; all rights remain with their respective holders.

`intro.wav` is linked by the archive as the victory / “Ride of the Valkyries” cue.
There is no separately labeled overall defeat recording, so the app uses the original
`player-hit.wav` cue for defeat.
