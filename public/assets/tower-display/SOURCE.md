# Original tower display artwork

These 21 JPEGs were downloaded from [The Dark Tower Page](https://well-of-souls.com/tower/)
on 2026-07-18. They correspond to the seven physical carousel positions and their three
lamp positions.

The archive identifies Bob Pepper as the game's original artist. It also says these images
were scanned from the instruction booklet and used without permission. No reuse license is
granted by the archive; all artwork and trademarks remain the property of their respective
rights holders.
